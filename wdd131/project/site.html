Heritage Connect

Preserving Family Stories for Future Generations

Site Name

Heritage Connect

This name represents a platform focused on connecting families to their history and heritage. It emphasizes unity, preservation, and learning across generations.

Optional domain availability: heritageconnect.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide tools and guidance for individuals and families who want to discover, document, and preserve their family history. The site will provide educational resources, story submission forms, and step-by-step research guidance.

Scenarios

  • How do I start researching my family history if I have no records?
  • Where can I submit and store my family stories online?
